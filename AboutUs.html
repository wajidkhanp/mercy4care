Mission & Vision Statement

Islamic Center of Laveen was created with the intention of achieving the following objectives:

  • Provide a secure and comfortable facility where Believers may gather for the worship of the one and only God ALLAH regardless of race, nationality, ethnicity, gender or age

  • Serve and to interact with the Muslim and Non-Muslim communities-at-large in accordance with the teachings of the Holy Quran and with the Sunna (way of life) of the prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him).

  • Provide religious and social services to all Muslims, render help to any person of similar persuasion in adapting to the Islamic culture and provide channels of understanding and communication between Muslims and the Arizona Community in general.

Our Vision is to define and convey a clear perception and understanding of the Islamic teachings to Muslims and Non-Muslims. We aim to inspire an appreciation of Islamic values by educating the community about the religion and its principles, and by demonstrating their effectiveness in today’s world. Our spiritual vision is to pursue faith, knowledge, justice, freedom, purpose, and righteousness in a peaceful and caring environment.
