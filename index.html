Mercy4Care Services, LLC is a caring behavioral health/substance abuse residential group home facility that provides independent living & social services to individuals that struggle with coping skills to live with daily challenges. We provide a secure environment in challenging times, A home with tranquility.
Mercy4Care Services partners with other agency’s in providing an individualized treatment plan tailored to meet the emotional, psychological, and physical needs of our residents. Our residents will receive compassionate care and be treated with the dignity and respect they deserve.
We know that for the average person, negative situations or feelings can usually be worked through. Yet, for someone suffering from depression or general mental health, those emotions are unmanageable, reflected through outlooks, through moods and through behaviors.
Unfortunately, the amount of people currently suffering from depression is very high. Due to this increase, many individuals see depression as the norm, as a harmless, easy to overcome illness. When in fact, it spans, across masses of diagnoses showcases the dangers and seriousness of the depressions as a mental health illness.
We strive to help our residents: 1. Improve their overall quality of life 2. Implement regular utilization of routine 3. Provide adequate health and social services and quality treatment options
