Our Behavior Health Facility Home

Our homes offer a safe, comfortable, family oriented living situations in residential neighborhoods throughout the state of Arizona.
Services are dedicated to promoting personal independence while protecting individuals from harm and respecting the dignity and lifestyle choices of our residents. Staff provide up to 24-hour care, 7 days a week, ensuring basic health & safety personal hygiene, self-awareness, social, and community skills development.
We offer unique, home environments with up to five persons per home, and we encourage active involvement in the neighborhood and surrounding community.
Our goal is to assist individuals in achieving personal accomplishments and be valued members of the community.

Our trained residential staff assist in the following areas:

  • Medical and Counseling Appointments
  • Prescriptions Filled
  • Behavior Treatment Plans
  • Emergency Situations
  • Specialized Nutritional and Dietary Requirements
  • Transportation to Life's Events
  • Assistance with Personal care
